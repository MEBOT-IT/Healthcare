Ambulance

An ambulance is a specially equipped vehicle designed to transport sick or injured people to medical facilities. It is staffed by trained medical professionals who can provide basic medical care during transport.

Types of Ambulances

  • Basic Life Support (BLS) Ambulance
  • Advanced Life Support (ALS) Ambulance
  • Neonatal Ambulance
  • Air Ambulance

Features of an Ambulance

  • Sirens and emergency lights
  • Oxygen and ventilation equipment
  • Stretchers and medical beds
  • Basic medical supplies such as bandages, splints, and medications

Contact Information

If you need to call an ambulance, dial 108

For non-emergency medical transportation, contact your local ambulance service.
